Dinah Bentley

Life Coaching
dinah@dinahbentleylifecoaching.co.uk

 

Life Coaching is a tried and tested technique which is interactive and goal-centric and is thus, for many people, a most effective way to bring new direction, understanding and fulfilment into your life. It is particularly suited to those in life where you may have any of the following feelings:

  • Have ideas /dreams but cannot make them happen
  • Feeling unhealthy /unfit / low energy / sick inside
  • Would like to talk to a partner or friends but do not know how
  • Find parenting and family life a struggle
  • Feeling lonely or alone either within a relationship or not
  • Recently bereaved
  • Recently divorced /separated

All parts of life are interwoven but at times the threads become muddled. When feeling lost and unsure it becomes a struggle to make the next step. This effects health, relationships, parenting, working environments and careers, no matter what status, age or gender.

  • Coaching encourages people to identify and follow their goals
  • Coaching facilitates learning, development and performance
  • Coaching develops skills, modifies behaviour and changes attitudes to find solutions
  • Coaching is both forward looking and holistic

About Dinah Bentley

Dinah brings to her coaching practice all her innate empathy and understanding of people and their situations. As a very intuitive and challenging coach, Dinah allows her clients to address their personal issues within the coaching framework. She will motivate and inspire clients to take the steps towards the way of life they may only have imagined.

Dinah qualified in 2002 with Distinction from the Coaching Academy and has a background in Nursing.

She has lived in both Brazil and South Africa and fully understands the impact a change of environment and culture makes on both individual and family life.
